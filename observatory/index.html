Public Nights for the Spring of 2014

Two Behlen Observatory public nights have been scheduled for the spring of 2014. Behlen Observatory will be open on public nights regardless of the weather and at least one presentation will be given. However, clouds and precipitation greatly will decrease our offerings. Visitors will be able to see the 30-inch telescope but will not be able to view astronomical objects through it when cloudy.

Date Time Event
Friday, March 7 7:30 pm to 10:00 pm Behlen Observatory Public Night
Friday, April 11 7:30 pm to 10:00 pm Behlen Observatory Public Night

The Student Observatory will NOT be open on cloudy evenings. In situations where you feel the conditions are borderline, you may call the student observatory at 472-4728 to verify that it is open. There will be 4 public nights at the Student Observatory during the spring of 2014.

Additional information may be available on the Student Observatory Twitter account https://twitter.com/ObservatoryUNL account.

Date Time Event
Friday, January 24 7:30 pm to 10:00 pm Student Observatory Public Night
Friday, February 21 7:30 pm to 10:00 pm Student Observatory Public Night
Friday, March 21 7:30 pm to 10:00 pm Student Observatory Public Night
Friday, April 18 7:30 pm to 10:00 pm Student Observatory Public Night
