I have capital O opinions about how journalism students should learn to analyze data. It involves code and replicable methods. It involves students with no experience writing code every class meeting. Because of that, it requires moving in small increments. And it requires data and questions relevant to their lives. So I’ve created free online textbooks for them, that you’ll find on this site.

Another thing I have capital O opinions about: I believe textbook prices are out of control. I’ll never charge my students for access to this material. If you use them and feel like you want to pay me for it, donate that money to support students in my college. It’ll do more good that way and it’s a tax write-off.
