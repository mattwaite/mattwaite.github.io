Key Highlights

A couple of milestones from my career at the intersection of news and technology.

Principal Developer, PolitiFact

In 2007, I was the principal developer for PolitiFact.com, a pioneering site for fact-checking political statements. In 2009, PolitiFact became the first website to be awarded the Pulitzer Prize for National Reporting.

Founder, Drone Journalism Lab

I founded the Drone Journalism Lab at UNL to explore the legal, ethical, and journalistic uses of drones. We train students to use emerging technologies safely and responsibly for newsgathering.
